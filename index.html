The Problem

African Logistics is Broken

Across Africa, logistics runs on phone calls, guesswork, and middlemen. Goods are delayed. Trucks sit idle. Sellers can't grow. We're here to change that.

🔍
Problem 01

Trucks Are Hard to Find

Businesses spend days making phone calls to find an available truck. Every hour lost is production delayed and money wasted.

🚛
Problem 02

Trucks Travel Empty

Trucks complete a delivery and return empty, burning fuel with nothing to show. Unmatched capacity that costs everyone more.

🏚️
Problem 03

Sellers Stuck in Local Markets

Physical market traders are invisible outside their street. No digital presence means no growth beyond whoever walks past their stall.

📍
Problem 04

Zero Delivery Visibility

Once goods leave, no one knows where they are. Calls. Waiting. Guessing. A broken chain of trust.

🤝
Problem 05

Middlemen Eat Your Margin

Transport brokers sit in the middle of every deal, adding markup without adding value. The buyer pays more. The driver earns less. Nobody wins.
